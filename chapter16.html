Chapter Sixteen

BEST OF LEADERS FOLLOW THE BEST LEADER EVER

Everyone knows that in order to be better at what you do, you need to learn from the best of teachers in that field. God is the best leader in the world. If you want to be a good leader to your youths, you have to do it hand in hand with Him.

The place of God in leadership cannot be overemphasized. He is the greatest leader. The best of leaders are those who follow God, the Best Leader Ever.

Leadership can be very easy when done with the assistance of God. On the other hand, it may be very tedious, monotonous and burdensome if the place of God is neglected.

As a leader who really desires to succeed, you will need to spend time with the Master Leader on daily basis. The magnitude of the task at hand and the urgency of what needs to be done may make you feel there is no such time to seek Godâ€™s face daily. The huge pressures on every side and the limited time available will want to make it difficult for you to spare any extra time to want to do this. You must understand that the time spent with God is not wasted time. It is time rightly invested and will yield serious dividends in your favor, from time to time.

Never deny yourself an opportunity to commune with Him daily. So many things will compete with this time-out with Him. Once you allow any of such things to override His leadership over your life, your influence and leadership over the people will begin a downward surge, which you may never recover from until you find your way back to Him.

A number of other things may look adequate enough to displace God from your life; the degrees you have, your personal

experience, the quality of your friends and advisers, the integrity of your work force, the good name you have among the people, the impact your leadership has made on your followers in so short a time, the recommendations you have from other leaders, the financial resources you control, the authority and power at your disposal, and a whole lot of others. However, none of all these is strong enough to take the place of God, neither are all of these put together, strong enough to do the same.

Of all your responsibilities as a youth leader, either to yourself or to your followers, this is the most important and the most significant one; your ability to follow the Best Leader Ever. When you begin to follow God, it will help you in so many areas.

GIVES YOU DIRECTION

So much has been said about the major functions of a youth leader, one of which is to give direction to his followers. What if the leader does not know which way to go? It becomes difficult to give the needed direction to the group. If he only depends on his five senses, or those of others, it is possible that he will mislead the group.

A leader who hears from God before taking actions will not find himself in this circumstance. God will, from time to time, give him direction on what to do and where to go.

As a youth leader, your ability to seek Godâ€™s face regarding the projects at hand will save you from a lot of problems and challenges lurking in the future for you.

HELPS YOUR IMPERFECTIONS

No one is perfect. Everyone has his own weaknesses which, though may not be obvious to all, but is best known to him, and to God, the Creator of all. Man may successfully hide his weaknesses from other men, but it makes no sense hiding it from the God who sees everything done in secret.

Leaders who go to God with their imperfections and weaknesses always receive help from Him. God can make all grace available if we trust Him to, but He does not interrupt until He is invited. Whatever situation you are passing through, God can help you out if you take it to Him.

As a youth leader, anytime you discover areas of your life where you still have issues, it could be a very obvious weakness, imperfection, or sins that easily beset, rather than try to cover them up and pretend all is well, take them to God in prayer. He is able, abundantly able, to help you. He will give you beauty in exchange for your ashes.

GIVES YOU DIVINE WISDOM

Do not be surprised. You may get confused as a leader. This could happen at the Frustration Stage of leadership. It may look as though all hell is let loose at you. All your professionalism, experience, ability and capability seem to work against you.

What you need at a time like this is not more of worldly knowledge. What you need is the wisdom of God. When the wisdom of God is released to you, you will know how to manage the seemingly impossible situation and God will be glorified through it.

Such display of superhuman wisdom will earn you a lot of respect and honor from your followers. Remember the fame of King Solomon began to spread the moment he engaged this supernatural wisdom to find out who the real mother of the living and the dead babies were. It will do same for you.

SAVES FROM IMPENDING DANGERS

God can also give you security beyond what law enforcement agents can do for you. He can save you from known and unknown impending dangers, if you trust Him to.

Youths could be naughty at times. Unknown to you, some may turn against you and decide to harm you. Besides the youths, other unseen forces may not be too pleased with the great work you are doing in the lives of these youths. They may get offended that you are saving destinies from the grip of hell and redirecting them to the kingdom of Godâ€™s dearest Son. For this reason, they may stage an attack. If you have God, they cannot succeed. God will see to it that you are divinely protected. He shall give His angels charge over you.

HELPS YOU TO ACHIEVE YOUR DREAM

At times, leaders bite much more than they can chew. They embark on such gargantuan projects that demand much more than they can ever raise, financially and otherwise. Then they get to the middle of it all and they are stuck; it becomes difficult to make progress and going back looks impossible.

At such times, for leaders who are willing to always allow the Best Leader to lead them, there is always a way out. Some unexplainable things just happen, and then progress becomes the only option. It could be that the Red Sea just parted, or the sun

just stood still. Whatever happens, help will surface at the nick of time. God may not come when He is needed, but He never comes late to those who place their trust in Him.

GIVES YOU STRENGTH

There may be moments when you will be so tired, fagged out, and will just feel like giving up. This is because human strength is limited. At such times, you need much more than the energy one derives from a balanced diet or an energy drink. What you need is grace. This grace is only made available by God.

If at any point in time you feel the magnitude of the task at hand is about to drive you crazy, the solution is not to give up. If you have a relationship with God, simply look up to heaven and demand for grace to continue. He will strengthen you and help you.

Even if you do not have a relationship with Him before now, if you turn to God today, He will save you, save your leadership, and save your organization. Simply bow your heart and invite Him into your life. Ask Him to forgive all your past sins. Request that He becomes your Lord, your Savior and your personal leader.

He will do exactly as you have requested, and much more than that, He will accept you as one of His sons and you will have full access to every divine blessing that the Father in heaven bestows on His children on earth.

Congratulations! You will discover that from now on, a certain level of grace that was, before now, unknown to you will begin to operate in your life.

CONCLUSION

These are just some of the many benefits of submitting your leadership to the Best Leader ever. If you have not done so, this is the best moment to, so that your tomorrow can be better than your today.
